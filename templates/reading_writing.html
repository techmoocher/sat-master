Reading Section

The SAT Reading section measures your ability to understand, analyze, and interpret complex texts across a range of disciplines. You'll encounter passages from literature, historical documents, social sciences, and natural sciences.

  • 65 minutes total
  • 5 passages with 10-11 questions each
  • 52 multiple-choice questions in total
