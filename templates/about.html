About SAT Master

Our Mission

At SAT Master, we dedicated to making high-quality SAT prep materials accessible to all students. Our mission is to equip students with necessary knowledge, skills, and strategies to achieve their dream scores on the SAT exam.

We believe that with the right resources and guidance, every student can improve their test-taking abilities and open doors to greater educational opportunities.

Why We Started

SAT Master was founded in 2025 by a group of dedicated students who recognized a gap in the test preparation market. We saw that many students could not afford expensive coaching centers or private tutors, yet needed more support than generic study guides could provide. Furthermore, many struggle in searching for necessary materials.

Our platform is created with an aim to bridging this gap, offering interactive, and comprehensive SAT preparation for everyone.

Our Approach to SAT Preparation

We believe effective SAT preparation involves three key components:

1
Content Mastery

Understanding the fundamental concepts and knowledge areas tested on the SAT

2
Strategy Development

Learning techniques specific to the SAT format and question types

3
Practice & Review

Applying knowledge through realistic practice and thorough analysis of mistakes
