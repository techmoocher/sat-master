What to Expect on the SAT Math Section

Understand the key areas tested on the SAT Math section

The SAT Math section tests your knowledge and skills in four key areas:

Heart of Algebra

Linear equations, systems of equations, and linear inequalities. These questions test your ability to analyze, fluently solve, and create linear equations and inequalities.

  • Solving linear equations and inequalities
  • Interpreting linear functions
  • Systems of linear equations

Problem-Solving & Data Analysis

Quantitative reasoning with ratios, percentages, and proportional reasoning. These questions test your ability to use ratios, proportions, percentages, and units to solve problems.

  • Percentages and ratios
  • Statistical analysis
  • Interpreting data from tables and graphs

Advanced Math

Manipulation of expressions and nonlinear equations. These questions test your ability to create, manipulate, and solve quadratic and higher-order equations.

  • Quadratic equations and functions
  • Polynomial manipulation
  • Exponential functions and growth

Geometry & Trigonometry

Problems involving area, volume, line, angle, and triangle relationships, as well as trigonometric functions. These questions test your understanding of shapes and angles.

  • Area and volume calculations
  • Coordinate geometry
  • Right triangle trigonometry
