Study by Topic

Algebra

Master essential algebraic concepts that represent about 35% of the SAT Math section:

  • Linear equations and inequalities
  • Quadratic and exponential functions
  • Systems of equations and their applications
  • Interpreting algebraic expressions in context
Start Now!

Problem Solving & Data Analysis

Develop skills in data interpretation and statistical reasoning that make up about 25% of the test:

  • Ratios, rates, proportions, and percentages
  • Analyzing and interpreting data in tables and graphs
  • Probability concepts and calculations
  • Statistical measures (mean, median, mode, range)
Start Now!

Trigonometry & Geometry

Understand spatial concepts and measurements encompassing approximately 20% of SAT math questions:

  • Angles, lines, triangles, circles, and polygons
  • Trigonometric functions and their applications
  • Area, volume, and coordinate geometry
  • Properties of triangles and the unit circle
Start Now!

Advanced Math

Master complex mathematical concepts that account for about 20% of the SAT Math questions:

  • Manipulating complex polynomial expressions
  • Function notation and interpretation
  • Nonlinear systems and complex equations
  • Advanced word problems and modeling situations
Start Now!
